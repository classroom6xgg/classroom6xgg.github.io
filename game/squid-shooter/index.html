    Description

    Dive into the thrilling underwater world with Squid Shooter, a high-octane action-adventure game that blends intense first-person shooting with strategic gameplay. Navigate through treacherous oceans, battling menacing sea creatures and rival squid factions. Utilize an array of customizable ink blasters and explosive gadgets to dominate the depths. With its stunning aquatic environments, immersive storyline, and multiplayer modes, Squid Shooter promises endless hours of adrenaline-pumping fun. Will you rise to become the ultimate underwater warrior, or will you be lost to the abyss?


    Instructions

    Objective: Defeat all the enemy squids and avoid obstacles.

    1. Starting the Game: Press the Start button to begin.

    2. Movement: Use the arrow keys to navigate your character:
    - Up Arrow to move up.
    - Down Arrow to move down.
    - Left Arrow to move left.
    - Right Arrow to move right.

    3. Shooting: Press the Spacebar to shoot ink at enemy squids.

    4. Power-ups: Collect power-ups scattered throughout the level to gain special abilities or extra points.

    5. Avoid Obstacles: Dodge any obstacles to prevent losing health.

    6. Health Meter: Keep an eye on your health meter at the top of the screen. If it depletes, the game is over.

    7. Level Progression: Clear all enemies to advance to the next level. Each level has progressively harder enemies and more obstacles.

    8. Winning the Game: Complete all levels to win.

    9. Pause/Resume: Press the P key to pause or resume the game.

    10. Score: Your score is displayed at the top of the screen. Defeat enemies and collect power-ups to increase your score.

    Enjoy playing Squid Shooter!
