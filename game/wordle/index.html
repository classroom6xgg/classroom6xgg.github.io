Description

Wordle is a captivating word puzzle game that challenges your vocabulary and logical thinking . Each day, you're given six chances to guess a mysterious five-letter word . With each guess, the game provides feedback: letters that are in the correct position, letters that are in the word but in the wrong position, and letters that aren't in the word at all. It's a race against time and your own wit as you strategically narrow down the possibilities. Perfect for fans of crosswords , anagram enthusiasts , and anyone who loves a good brain teaser , Wordle is the daily mental workout you've been searching for. Sharpen your language skills and enjoy the thrill of cracking the code!


Instructions

Wordle is a word puzzle game where the objective is to guess a hidden 5-letter word within 6 attempts .

1. Start by entering any 5-letter word .
2. After each guess, the letters in your word will change colors to provide clues:
- Green : The letter is in the correct position.
- Yellow : The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
- Gray : The letter is not in the word at all.
3. Use the color feedback to refine your next guess. You have a total of 6 attempts to guess the hidden word correctly.
4. The game ends either when you guess the word correctly or when you run out of attempts.

Enjoy solving the puzzle!
