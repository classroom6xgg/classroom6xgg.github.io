Description

Leap into a world of high-stakes adventure with Frogger! Guide your fearless amphibian hero across treacherous highways and raging rivers in a quest to reach the safety of home. Dodge speeding cars, hop across floating logs, and outmaneuver snapping turtles in this classic arcade game that tests your reflexes and timing . With vibrant graphics and challenging levels, Frogger offers endless excitement for players of all ages. Will you master the journey or become roadkill? The challenge awaits!


Instructions

In Frogger , your goal is to guide a frog from the bottom to the top of the screen, avoiding obstacles. Use the arrow keys to move the frog up, down, left, or right .

1. Start at the bottom of the screen.
2. Move the frog across the road, avoiding oncoming traffic like cars and trucks.
3. Reach the median safely, then continue to the river .
4. Cross the river by jumping on logs, turtles, and other floating objects without falling into the water.
5. Avoid predators and hazards in the river.
6. Reach one of the safe zones at the top of the screen to advance to the next level.

Complete all levels while keeping the frog safe to win the game.
