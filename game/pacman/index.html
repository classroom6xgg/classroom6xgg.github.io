Description

Action : Dive into the fast-paced world of Pacman , where quick reflexes and strategic movements are your best allies. Navigate intricate mazes, evade relentless ghosts, and gobble up power pellets to turn the tables on your pursuers. Every second counts in this thrilling game of chase!

Adventure : Embark on a nostalgic journey with Pacman , exploring a series of vibrant mazes filled with secrets and surprises. As you munch your way through the labyrinth, uncover hidden paths and outsmart cunning ghosts in this timeless adventure.

Puzzle : Challenge your mind with Pacman , where every move counts. Strategically navigate complex mazes, plan your routes to collect all the pellets, and use power-ups wisely to outmaneuver the ever-persistent ghosts. It's a puzzle of precision and timing!

Arcade : Step into the classic arcade world of Pacman , where the iconic yellow hero races through mazes to chomp down pellets and evade colorful ghosts. With its easy-to-learn, hard-to-master gameplay, Pacman promises endless hours of fun and competition.

Survival : Test your endurance in Pacman , where survival is the name of the game. Navigate through increasingly challenging mazes, avoid the relentless ghosts, and see how long you can last. Each pellet you eat brings you one step closer to victory!

Strategy : Master the art of evasion and capture in Pacman . Develop strategies to outwit the ghostly enemies, make split-second decisions, and plan your path to clear each maze efficiently. Every level requires cunning and tactical thinking to succeed.


Instructions

Objective : Navigate Pacman through the maze to eat all the dots while avoiding the ghosts .

Controls :
- Arrow Keys : Move Pacman up, down, left, or right .

Gameplay :
1. Start the game and use the arrow keys to control Pacman.
2. Eat all the small and large dots scattered throughout the maze.
3. Avoid the colorful ghosts that move around the maze. If a ghost catches Pacman, you lose a life.
4. Power Pellets (larger dots) temporarily turn the ghosts blue , allowing Pacman to eat them for extra points.
5. Collect fruits and other bonus items for additional points.

Winning : Clear all the dots from the maze to advance to the next level.

Losing : Lose all your lives by being caught by the ghosts.
