Description

Embark on a nostalgic journey with Google Snake , a rejuvenated twist on the classic arcade experience! Navigate your ever-growing snake through a vibrant digital landscape, collecting colorful fruits and avoiding collisions with the walls or your own tail. With each bite, your snake gets longer and the challenge intensifies. How long can you survive and how high can you score? Dive into this addictive game and test your reflexes and strategy in endless gameplay that promises both fun and frustration in equal measure!


Instructions

In Google Snake , you control a snake that moves around the screen to eat apples . Here’s how to play:

1. Start the Game : Open Google and search for “Google Snake” to launch the game.
2. Control the Snake : Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the snake up, down, left, or right .
3. Eat Apples : Guide the snake to eat the apples that appear on the screen. Each apple eaten makes the snake grow longer.
4. Avoid Collisions : Do not let the snake run into the walls or its own tail , as this will end the game.
5. Score Points : Your score increases with each apple you eat. Try to eat as many apples as possible to achieve a high score.

Enjoy the game and see how long you can grow your snake!
