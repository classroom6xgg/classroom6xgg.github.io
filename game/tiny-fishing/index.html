Description

Tiny Fishing is an adorable and relaxing fishing adventure where you take on the role of a mini fisherman, exploring tranquil lakes and hidden ponds. Cast your line, reel in a variety of vibrant fish, and upgrade your gear to catch even more impressive aquatic creatures. Perfect for casual gamers, Tiny Fishing offers a soothing experience with charming graphics and simple yet addictive gameplay, making it easy to dive into the fun without needing extensive fishing knowledge. Enjoy the serenity and joy of fishing anytime, anywhere!


Instructions

In Tiny Fishing , your goal is to catch as many fish as possible to earn coins and upgrade your fishing gear.

1. Cast your line : Click or tap the screen to lower your fishing line into the water.
2. Reel in fish : Drag your line to move it left or right, aiming to hook fish.
3. Avoid obstacles : Watch out for rocks and other underwater obstacles that can block your line.
4. Earn coins : The more fish you collect, the more coins you earn.
5. Upgrade gear : Use coins to upgrade your fishing rod, line length, and bait to catch bigger and more valuable fish.
6. Repeat : Keep fishing and upgrading to catch
