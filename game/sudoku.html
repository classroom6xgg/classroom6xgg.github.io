    Description

    Dive into the mind-bending world of Sudoku, the ultimate puzzle game that challenges your logic and problem-solving skills. Fill the 9x9 grid with numbers, ensuring each column, row, and 3x3 subgrid contains the digits 1 through 9 without repetition. With varying levels of difficulty, Sudoku offers endless hours of fun and brain-training for both beginners and seasoned enthusiasts. Sharpen your mind and embark on a journey of numerical mastery with this classic intellectual challenge.


    Instructions

    Sudoku is a number puzzle game played on a 9x9 grid divided into nine 3x3 subgrids. The objective is to fill the grid so that every row, every column, and every 3x3 subgrid contains the numbers 1 to 9 exactly once.

    1. Start with a partially filled grid containing some numbers already in place.
    2. Examine the empty cells and determine which numbers are missing from the row, column, and subgrid.
    3. Place a number in an empty cell, ensuring it does not duplicate any number in the same row, column, or 3x3 subgrid.
    4. Continue filling in numbers using logic and elimination until the entire grid is filled correctly.
    5. Check your work to ensure all rows, columns, and subgrids contain each number from 1 to 9 exactly once.

    Tips:
    - Use pencil marks to note possible numbers for each cell.
    - Look for patterns and use process of elimination to narrow down choices.
    - Stay patient and take your time to avoid mistakes.
