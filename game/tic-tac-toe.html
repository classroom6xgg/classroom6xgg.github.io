    Description

    Tic Tac Toe is a classic strategy game that pits two players against each other in a battle of wits and foresight. Perfect for quick bursts of casual fun, it's easy to learn but challenging to master. Players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid, aiming to be the first to align three of their symbols, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Whether you're playing on paper, a digital screen, or a physical board, Tic Tac Toe provides endless opportunities for brain-teasing competition. Sharpen your tactical skills and enjoy the timeless appeal of this versatile and interactive game.


    Instructions

    Objective: Be the first player to get 3 of your marks in a row either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

    Setup: Draw a 3x3 grid.

    Players: 2 players. One is X and the other is O.

    Starting: Decide who goes first. Players take turns.

    How to Play:
    1. On your turn, place your mark (X or O) in an empty cell on the grid.
    2. Alternate turns with your opponent.

    Winning: The first player to align 3 of their marks in a row wins.

    Draw: If all 9 cells are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a draw.
