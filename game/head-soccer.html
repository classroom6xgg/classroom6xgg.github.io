    Description

    Head Soccer is an exhilarating sports game that blends the thrill of soccer with quirky, fast-paced action. Choose from a variety of unique, animated characters, each with their own special abilities, and compete in intense one-on-one matches. Use your character's head to score goals and unleash powerful moves to outplay your opponent. With easy-to-learn controls and a range of exciting game modes, Head Soccer offers endless fun for players of all ages. Dive into the arena, perfect your timing, and become the ultimate Head Soccer champion!


    Instructions

    Objective: Score more goals than your opponent within the game time.

    Setup:
    1. Select your character and customize its appearance.
    2. Choose a game mode (e.g., tournament, friendly match).

    Controls:
    - Arrow keys to move.
    - Spacebar to jump.
    - X key to kick the ball.
    - Z key for a special move (when available).

    Gameplay:
    1. Kick off starts the game. Use the arrow keys to position your character.
    2. Score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's net.
    3. Defend your goal using jumps and kicks.
    4. Use power-ups (if available) to gain an advantage.

    Winning:
    - The player with the most goals when the timer runs out wins the match.
    - In case of a tie, the game may go into extra time or a penalty shootout.
