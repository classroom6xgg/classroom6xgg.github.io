    Description

    Crossy Road is an endless arcade hopper that tests your skills and reflexes as you guide your character across busy roads, treacherous rivers, and bustling train tracks. Avoid oncoming traffic, leap over logs, and dodge speeding trains to see just how far you can go. With its charming voxel art style, hilarious characters, and addictive gameplay, Crossy Road offers endless fun for players of all ages. Collect coins to unlock quirky new characters and challenge your friends to beat your high score in this fast-paced, endlessly entertaining adventure!


    Instructions

    Crossy Road is an endless arcade game where you navigate your character through various obstacles.

    1. Start the Game: Open the app and tap the screen to begin.
    2. Move Forward: Tap the screen to move your character one step forward.
    3. Change Direction: Swipe left or right to move sideways, and swipe down to move backward.
    4. Avoid Obstacles: Dodge cars, trains, and other hazards. Stay clear of water unless you can jump on logs or lily pads.
    5. Collect Coins: Gather coins along the way to unlock new characters.
    6. Stay Alert: Keep an eye on the moving traffic and time your moves carefully.
    7. Endless Play: Continue moving as far as possible without getting hit or falling into the water.

    Remember, the objective is to see how far you can go while avoiding obstacles and collecting coins. Enjoy and have fun!
