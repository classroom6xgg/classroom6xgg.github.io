Description

Getaway Shootout is an exhilarating action-packed game where you and your friends race through chaotic urban environments to reach the getaway vehicle first. Navigate through obstacle-filled maps using unique physics mechanics, collect powerful weapons and power-ups , and outsmart your opponents in this fast-paced, competitive multiplayer experience. With its quirky characters and unpredictable gameplay, Getaway Shootout guarantees endless fun and laughter for everyone!


Instructions

Getaway Shootout is a fun, action-packed game where you race against opponents to reach the getaway vehicle. Here's how to play:

1. Start the Game : Choose your character and select a map.
2. Controls :
- Player 1 : Use W to jump left, E to jump right, and R for power-ups.
- Player 2 : Use I to jump left, O to jump right, and P for power-ups.
3. Objective : Navigate through the level, avoiding obstacles and opponents, to be the first to reach the getaway vehicle.
4. Power-ups : Collect various power-ups scattered throughout the map to gain advantages, such as weapons or speed boosts.
5. Winning : Reach the getaway vehicle first to win the round. The game is typically played in multiple rounds.

Have fun and enjoy the chaotic race to your escape!
