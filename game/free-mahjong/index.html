    Description

    Dive into the timeless classic with Free Mahjong, where strategy meets serenity. This puzzle game challenges you to clear the board by matching pairs of captivating tiles. Navigate through beautifully designed layouts, each offering a unique test of your pattern recognition skills. Perfect for both casual players and avid enthusiasts, Free Mahjong offers an immersive experience with relaxing music and soothing visuals. Whether you’re aiming to beat your own high score or simply unwind, this game provides endless hours of engaging entertainment. Ready to master the art of tile-matching?


    Instructions

    Free Mahjong is a tile-matching game that requires strategy and concentration. Here’s how to play:

    1. Set Up: The game begins with a layout of 144 tiles arranged in a specific pattern. The tiles feature different symbols and images.

    2. Objective: The goal is to match pairs of identical tiles and remove them from the board, ultimately clearing all tiles.

    3. Selecting Tiles: Click on a tile to select it. Only the "free" tiles can be selected (tiles that are not covered by others and have at least one side (left or right) open).

    4. Matching Tiles: Find and click on another tile that matches the selected tile. If both tiles are free, they will be removed from the board.

    5. Strategy: Plan your moves to avoid blocking tiles that you may need later. Focus on matching tiles that will free up the most options.

    6. Winning the Game: Continue matching and removing tiles until all tiles are cleared. If no more moves are possible, the game ends.

    7. Hints and Undo: Use the Hint button if you get stuck, or the Undo button to reverse your last move.
