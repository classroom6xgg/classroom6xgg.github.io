    Description

    Tomb of the Mask is a retro arcade adventure where you don a mysterious mask that grants you incredible powers. Navigate through a labyrinthine dungeon filled with traps and enemies as you climb walls, dodge spikes, and outsmart sinister creatures. With endless procedurally generated levels, each run offers a fresh challenge, testing your reflexes and strategic thinking. Collect coins and power-ups to boost your abilities and uncover the secrets hidden within the ancient tomb. Get ready for a fast-paced, pixel-art journey where every move counts!


    Instructions

    Tomb of the Mask is an arcade game where you navigate a maze to collect dots and avoid obstacles.

    1. Launch the game and tap the screen to start.
    2. Swipe in any direction (up, down, left, or right) to move your character.
    3. Collect dots scattered throughout the maze for points.
    4. Avoid obstacles such as spikes, enemies, and traps.
    5. Power-ups can be collected for special abilities or invincibility.
    6. Reach the exit of each level to progress to the next one.
    7. Complete objectives and challenges to earn additional rewards.

    Keep moving quickly and strategically to outmaneuver dangers and complete the maze!
