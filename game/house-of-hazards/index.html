    Description

    House of Hazards is an action-packed, multiplayer game where players navigate a seemingly ordinary house filled with hidden dangers and traps. Each room presents new challenges that test your reflexes and strategy. Work together or compete against friends in this chaotic environment to complete everyday tasks like making breakfast or watering plants, all while avoiding unexpected hazards like falling chandeliers, slippery floors, and mischievous pets. With its dynamic gameplay, hilarious scenarios, and colorful graphics, House of Hazards guarantees to keep you on your toes and laughing for hours. Get ready to experience the most unpredictable house ever!


    Instructions

    In House of Hazards, your goal is to navigate through a house filled with traps while completing various tasks. Follow these steps:

    1. Select Your Character: Choose from a variety of quirky characters.
    2. Objective: Each level has specific tasks to complete, such as making coffee, watering plants, or checking mail.
    3. Avoid Hazards: Watch out for traps set by other players, such as swinging lights, falling objects, and water sprays.
    4. Movement: Use the arrow keys to move your character around the house.
    5. Interact: Press the space bar to interact with objects and complete tasks.
    6. Multiplayer: Play with friends and take turns setting traps or trying to complete tasks.
    7. Winning: The player who successfully completes all their tasks first without getting caught in traps wins the game.

    Be strategic in setting traps and quick in avoiding them to achieve victory!
