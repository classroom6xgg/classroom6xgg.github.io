Description

Squid Game is a thrilling survival game where players are thrown into a series of high-stakes challenges that test their wits, strength, and strategy . Each round brings a new twist, with classic childhood games turning deadly. In this intense, psychological horror experience, alliances form and shatter as competitors vie for a massive cash prize. Can you outlast the competition and emerge victorious, or will you fall victim to the game's sinister traps? Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping ride where your every move could be your last.


Instructions

Objective : Be the last player standing by successfully completing all the games.

Players : Multiple participants.

Games :

1. Red Light, Green Light :
- Players must move towards the finish line when the call is “Green Light.”
- Players must stop moving immediately when the call is “Red Light.”
- Any player caught moving during “Red Light” is eliminated.

2. Honeycomb :
- Each player receives a honeycomb candy with a shape imprinted.
- Players must carve out the shape without breaking it.
- Breaking the shape results in elimination.

3. Tug of War :
- Players are divided into two teams.
- Teams pull on a rope to drag the opposing team across the center line.
- The team that crosses the center line loses and is eliminated.

4. Marbles :
- Players are paired up and given 10 marbles each.
- Players must win all the opponent's marbles through any agreed game (e.g., guessing odd/even).
- The player who loses all their marbles is eliminated.

5. Glass Stepping Stones :
- Players must cross a bridge made of glass panels.
- Some panels are strong while others will break under pressure.
- Players must choose the correct panels to avoid falling and being eliminated.

Winning : The last player remaining after all games is the winner.
